Job Description

Google is looking for a talented and motivated Python Developer to join our dynamic engineering team. As a Python Developer at Google, you will design, develop, and optimize scalable software solutions, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and drive the development of innovative projects.

Responsibilities

  • Develop robust, scalable, and efficient software applications using Python.
  • Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product managers, designers, and other developers, to deliver innovative solutions.
  • Debug, troubleshoot, and improve existing systems and processes.
  • Implement APIs and integrate third-party services.
  • Stay updated with emerging trends and technologies in Python development and software engineering.

Qualifications

Required:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
  • 3-5 years of professional experience in Python development.
  • Strong understanding of Python frameworks (e.g., Django, Flask, FastAPI).
  • Experience with cloud platforms such as Google Cloud, AWS, or Azure.
  • Proficiency in version control systems like Git.
  • Solid understanding of databases (SQL and NoSQL).
  • Knowledge of RESTful APIs and microservices architecture.
  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills.
